1. Acceptance of These Terms

By reading, joining, lurking, posting, reacting, replying, quoting, or otherwise interacting with the 5-A Inc. group chat (the “Service”), you agree to comply with these Terms of Service (“Terms”).

If you do not agree, your remedy is simple and ancient: do not use the Service.

2. Eligibility & Access

Access to the Service is granted at the sole and dramatic discretion of 5-A Inc. moderators (“Mods”). Mods may remove access at any time for any reason related to chat health, safety, or vibes.

2.1 Account Responsibility

  • You are responsible for what you post from your account/device.
  • “My cousin took my phone” is not a magical shield.

3. Acceptable Use (Behavior Rules)

You agree to use the Service like a normal person in a shared room with other normal people. Specifically, you will not:

  • Harass, threaten, or repeatedly target users.
  • Use slurs, hate speech, or dehumanizing language.
  • Start fights on purpose, bait arguments, or “farm” reactions.
  • Share explicit sexual content, especially involving minors (instant ban, no exceptions).
  • Encourage self-harm, violence, or illegal activity.
  • Impersonate Mods, members, or “official” accounts.

3.1 Respect the Room

4. Anti-Spam Policy (Zero Fun Allowed for Spammers)

Spamming is prohibited. “Spamming” includes, but is not limited to:

  • Sending repeated messages rapidly to flood the chat.
  • Copy/pasting the same text, emojis, or images repeatedly.
  • Chain messages, “send this to 10 people,” or similar nonsense.
  • Unsolicited advertisements, self-promo dumps, or “join my server” blasts.
  • Bot-like behavior or automated posting.
Spam is a fast-track enforcement category.

The Service may apply immediate restrictions (mute/kick/ban) without warning if spam is clear.

4.1 Reasonable Posting

  • If you have 8 thoughts, you can put them in one message. Revolutionary, I know.
  • Use @mentions sparingly; mass pings are treated like spam.

5. Content Rules

5.1 NSFW / Gore / Shock Content

  • No pornographic content.
  • No graphic gore, animal cruelty, or shock content.
  • No posting real violence for laughs.

5.2 Personal Information (Doxxing)

  • Do not share anyone’s private info: full names, addresses, phone numbers, school details, IPs, etc.
  • Do not “hint” private info or encourage others to dig it up.

5.3 Illegal / Risky Content

  • No sharing malware, phishing links, or instructions intended to harm others’ devices/accounts.
  • No scamming, fraud, or “free nitro” type stuff.

5.4 Copyright & Credits

  • Don’t repost others’ work and claim it’s yours.
  • Crediting is good. Permission is better.

6. Privacy & Screenshot Policy

The Service is not private, but it is also not a public “content farm.” Use basic respect.

  • Do not screenshot messages to start drama elsewhere.
  • Do not share screenshots containing private info.
  • If you want to quote someone outside the chat, ask first (unless it’s clearly harmless).

Mods may request deletion of posts/screenshots that cause harm or expose private information.

7. Moderation & Enforcement

Mods may interpret and enforce these Terms to keep the Service usable. Mods may:

  • Remove messages (where platform features allow).
  • Warn, mute, kick, restrict, or ban users.
  • Set slowmode, limit media, or lock channels temporarily.

7.1 “We Were Joking” Clause

If the impact is harmful, “it was a joke” does not automatically make it okay.

8. Penalties

Penalties vary based on severity, repetition, intent, and whether you’re being weird about it. Typical escalation may include:

  1. Friendly warning
  2. Formal warning
  3. Temporary mute/restriction
  4. Kick
  5. Temporary ban
  6. Permanent ban (aka “The Door”)

Some violations can skip steps and go straight to a ban (e.g., hate speech, doxxing, explicit content involving minors, credible threats).

9. Appeals (aka “I’m Not Guilty”)

If you believe a moderation action was incorrect, you may appeal by contacting a Mod calmly.

  • Explain what happened, briefly.
  • Don’t spam Mods (that makes it worse).
  • Harassing Mods about a decision may result in extended penalties.

10. Changes to These Terms

5-A Inc. may update these Terms when needed (new chaos drops, new rules appear). Continued use of the Service after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Terms.

11. Contact

For questions, appeals, or reports, contact a moderator in the group chat.
